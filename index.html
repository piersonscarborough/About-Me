ABOUT ME

Hi! I'm Pierson Scarborough

I'm a designer, a developer, and a creator. Before becoming a web developer, I was in advertising, focused primarily on content creation and design. If you had asked me two years ago if I thought I would be coding and building applications today, I would’ve thought you were crazy. Computer Science always on the surface appeared to be reserved for those who excelled in mathematics and/or science, not necessarily creatives. As I saw the market gradually shifting towards the web and more and more companies were looking for designers with the ability to code, I decided to dive into this challenging discipline headfirst and haven’t looked back.

The world of development is just another outlet for me to build and create. I believe there is great value in not only being able to understand design, but also being able to create and implement it through code. Just like designing a logo or developing pictures, I find the coding process to be therapeutic. The many intricate steps it takes to build something makes the end product that much more gratifying.

When I’m not writing code, you can find me shooting photography, creating art, thrifting, and just being active outdoors. I also offer freelance design and photography on the side. Contact me and let’s build something!
